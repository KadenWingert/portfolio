Nordland Forge

Description

Nordland Forge, a React and MongoDB-powered website for a small blade forging business, seamlessly integrates CRUD operations. It enables the business owner to easily add, update, and manage handforged blades in the inventory, providing customers with a dynamic and user-friendly browsing experience. React ensures real-time updates, while MongoDB handles secure transaction details during checkout. This efficient use of CRUD operations enhances both the business owner's management capabilities and the customer's experience.
